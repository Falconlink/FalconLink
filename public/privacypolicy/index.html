FalconLink does not collect, store, or share any of the following information:

Since FalconLink does not collect any personal data, there is no personal information at risk. We operate under a policy of zero-logging, which means we do not track or store any user data. Your privacy is protected because we don't retain information that could be used to identify you.

Please review the privacy policies of these third parties for more information.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure that your browsing experience is safe while using FalconLink. Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to protect. However, we still use encryption and secure connections to safeguard your anonymity.
